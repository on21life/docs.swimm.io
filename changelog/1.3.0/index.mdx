# Swimm 1.3.0 has been released

We're pleased to announce the availability of **Swimm 1.3.0**<SwimmMoji text="release"/>

## New Features:
- **Edit doc** - You can now switch branches while editing a document, using the branch selector or through the 'Commit changes' button.

## Improvements:
- **Opening pages in a new tab** -  You can now open repo pages in a new tab across the app.
- **Search performance** - Improved.

For further information and any questions, please reach out to us on our <SwimmLink target="slack" />.
